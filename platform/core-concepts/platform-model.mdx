## Platform Model

Coline is built on three foundational layers: Primitives, Files, and Containers.

## Primitives

Atomic data types: message, string, number, url, file, date, user, boolean.

## Files

Every piece of content in Coline is a typed file.

## Containers

Where files live: Workspaces, Drives, Channels, DMs, Calendar Events.
